About Us

Welcome to Sunshine Orphanage, a place where love, care, and hope flourish. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for children who need it the most.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a safe haven for orphaned and abandoned children, ensuring they receive the love, care, and education they need to grow into happy and successful adults.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has a loving and supportive environment, empowering them to reach their full potential and lead fulfilling lives.

Our History

Founded in 2000, Sunshine Orphanage has been a beacon of hope for children in need. Over the past two decades, we have grown from a small shelter to a comprehensive care facility, thanks to the support of our community and dedicated staff.
